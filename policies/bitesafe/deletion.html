Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 10, 2026

BiteSafe does not store any data on remote servers. All your data is stored locally on your device. There is no server-side data to delete.

What Data Does BiteSafe Store?

The following data is stored only on your device in a local SQLite database:

  • Scan history (barcodes you've scanned and their scores)
  • Favorite products
  • Cached product data from Open Food Facts
  • Family member profiles and dietary preferences
  • Grocery list items
  • App settings (theme, language, onboarding status)

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Clear App Data (keep the app installed)

  1. Open your device's Settings
  2. Go to Apps (or "Apps & Notifications")
  3. Find and tap BiteSafe
  4. Tap Storage & Cache
  5. Tap Clear Storage (or "Clear Data")

This deletes all local data including scan history, favorites, preferences, and cached products. The app will behave as if freshly installed.

Option 2: Uninstall the App

  1. Long-press the BiteSafe icon on your home screen
  2. Tap Uninstall

Uninstalling the app automatically removes all local data from your device.

Third-Party Data

  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous usage statistics. This data is not personally identifiable and is automatically deleted after 14 months per Google's retention policy.
  • Google AdMob — ad-related data is managed by Google. You can control ad personalization in your Google Account settings.
  • RevenueCat — if you purchased a subscription, purchase records are managed by Google Play. You can manage subscriptions in the Google Play Store.

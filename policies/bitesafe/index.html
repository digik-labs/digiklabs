Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

BiteSafe ("the App") is a food product scanner that helps you understand the health profile of packaged foods. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

BiteSafe is designed with a privacy-first approach. We collect minimal data:

  • Camera access — Used solely to scan product barcodes and photograph ingredient labels. Images are processed on-device and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Photo library access — Used only when you choose to pick a barcode image from your gallery. Images are not stored or transmitted.
  • Internet access — Used to fetch product data from the Open Food Facts public database. No personal data is sent in these requests.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only using SQLite:

  • Scan history (products you've scanned)
  • Favorite products
  • Dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, diabetic)
  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Onboarding completion status

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Open Food Facts API — To retrieve product information. Their privacy policy is available at openfoodfacts.org/privacy.
  • Google AdMob — To display banner advertisements in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Premium subscribers do not see ads.
  • Google ML Kit (on-device) — For text recognition (OCR) of ingredient labels. All processing happens on-device; no data is sent to Google's servers.

4. Personal Information

BiteSafe does not:

  • Require account creation or login
  • Collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • Track your location
  • Share or sell any data to third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking SDKs (other than AdMob for ad serving)

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. In-App Purchases

BiteSafe offers an optional premium subscription. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Privacy Policy

Med Saathi — Your Family's Health Companion
Last updated: March 1, 2026

Digik Labs ("we", "our", or "us") built Med Saathi as a health management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you sign up, we collect your name and email address through Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, or email/password registration. Authentication is handled securely via Supabase.

b) Health & Medical Data

  • Medication details (names, dosages, schedules, adherence logs)
  • Vitals data (blood pressure, blood sugar, and other health metrics you enter)
  • Medical reports you upload (PDF, JPG, PNG files)
  • ABHA health ID and linked health records (if you choose to integrate)

c) Family Data

If you create or join a family group, we store family member profiles and share health data between authorized family members as configured by you.

d) Payment Information

Subscription payments are processed through Razorpay. We do not store your credit/debit card details. Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay under their own privacy policy.

e) Device Permissions

  • Camera & Storage: Used to capture or select medical reports for upload
  • Notifications: Used for medication reminders and health alerts via Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Biometric Authentication: Used for local app lock (fingerprint/face). Biometric data never leaves your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide medication tracking, reminders, and adherence monitoring
  • To store and organize your medical reports
  • To enable family health management and data sharing between authorized members
  • To send medication reminders and health alert notifications
  • To process subscription payments
  • To improve and maintain the app

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (cloud-hosted PostgreSQL). Uploaded files (medical reports, images) are stored in Supabase Storage with row-level security policies ensuring only authorized users can access their data.

We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or health data with third parties, except:

  • Family members: Health data is shared within your family group as configured by you
  • Service providers: Supabase (data hosting), Firebase (notifications), Razorpay (payments) — these providers process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Advertising & Analytics

Med Saathi does not contain any advertisements. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. Upon deletion, all your personal data, health records, and uploaded files will be permanently removed from our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Med Saathi is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Family members may add health profiles for minors under parental supervision.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Correct inaccurate data via the app's profile and settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your health data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
